# Scrollytelling Landing Page (Anti-Gravity Scroll)

### Global Behavior
- Scroll controls time
- Scroll down → forward
- Scroll up → rewind
- Visual fixed and centered
- No autoplay
- All motion is scroll-driven

### Visual Anchor
- Use cinematic portrait sequence (240 frames)
- Subject fixed (head to waist)
- No camera movement
- No scale, rotation, or parallax
- Frame-by-frame scroll mapping

### Scene Duration
- Total frames: 240
- Total scroll sections: 5
